About Me

Hi, I’m Billy Aguinaga — a front-end developer with a full-stack foundation and a background in Chemistry that shapes how I approach systems, design, and problem-solving.

This site is both portfolio and project — built with Angular and integrated with Three.js as a creative challenge to push beyond the familiar. What began as a visual experiment evolved into a 3D canvas that blends interactivity, symbolism, and deliberate architecture.

For me, development is a convergence of engineering and creative intent. Practically, I care about accessibility, performance, and usability—but also about building interfaces that feel considered. Not just functional, but meaningful. Something someone cared about.

After stepping away from full-time development, I’ve returned with renewed clarity and a focused trajectory. I’m currently expanding into React and Next.js with a focus on projects that grow my skill set and reflect both utility and intent.
